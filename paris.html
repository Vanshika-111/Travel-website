History

Ancient Origins

3rd century BCE: Paris was originally a settlement of a Celtic tribe called the Parisii, located on the Île de
la Cité (now part of central Paris).52 BCE: Conquered by the Romans and renamed Lutetia (Lutetia Parisiorum).

Medieval Paris

Became an important religious and commercial center in the Middle Ages.Construction of iconic landmarks like Notre-Dame
Cathedral began in the 12th century.around the Seine River, with a bustling university district known as the Latin Quarter.

The French Revolution (1789)

Paris was the epicenter of revolutionary activity that overthrew the monarchy.Events like the storming of the Bastille
and the Reign of Terror happened here.

20th Century - From War to World Stage

Under Emperor Napoleon III, Baron Haussmann redesigned Paris with wide boulevards, parks, and modern sewer systems.
Occupied during World War II by Nazi Germany; liberated in 1944.Post-war, Paris grew as a center for diplomacy,
culture, and tourism.key global events and movements including the Paris Peace Accords and May 1968 student protests.
