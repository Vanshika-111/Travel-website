History

Indigenous Roots

Originally inhabited by the Eora Nation, including the Gadigal people.


Convict Era (1788-1840s)

Thousands of convicts were transported from Britain and built much of early Sydney.

1850s-1900s

Gold rush era brought economic boom and immigration; Sydney expanded rapidly.

Post-WWII Boom

Massive immigration shaped modern Sydney into a global multicultural city.

Modern Icon

Home to the Opera House (1973) and Harbour Bridge (1932), now symbols of Australia.
