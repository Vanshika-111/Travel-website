Tokyo

Tradition Meets Technology in the Heart of Tokyo

About

Tokyo is a dazzling blend of ancient tradition and futuristic innovation, where serene temples meet neon-lit skyscrapers.
From sushi streets to tech hubs, the city pulses with energy, culture, and creativity.

History:


Ancient Name

Tokyo was originally a small fishing village called Edo, which means "estuary".


Rise to Power

In 1603, Tokugawa Ieyasu established the Tokugawa Shogunate, making Edo the political center of Japan.


Meiji Restoration (1868)

Edo was renamed Tokyo, meaning "Eastern Capital", as the Emperor moved from Kyoto to Tokyo.

Modern Development

After being devastated by the Great Kanto Earthquake (1923) and World War II bombings,Tokyo rebuilt itself
into one of the most advanced cities in the world.
